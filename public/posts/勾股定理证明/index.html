证明 bc^2 + cg^2 = bg^2

证:

>∵ab=bc,be=bg,∠abg=∠ebc> > \because ab = bc, be = bg, \angle abg = \angle ebc > >∵ab=bc,be=bg,∠abg=∠ebc>

>∴△abg≅△ebc> > \therefore \triangle abg \cong \triangle ebc > >∴△abg≅△ebc>

>∵2×S△abg=S□abcd> > \because 2 \times S\triangle abg = S\square abcd > >∵2×S△abg=S□abcd>

>2×S△ebc=S□bekj> > 2 \times S\triangle ebc = S\square bekj > >2×S△ebc=S□bekj>

>∴S□abcd=S□bekj> > \therefore S\square abcd = S\square bekj > >∴S□abcd=S□bekj>

同理可得:

>S□cghi=S□gfkj> > S\square cghi = S\square gfkj > >S□cghi=S□gfkj>

>∴S□abcd+S□cghi=S□befg> > \therefore S\square abcd + S\square cghi = S\square befg > >∴S□abcd+S□cghi=S□befg>

>∴bc2+cg2=bg2> > \therefore bc^2 + cg^2 = bg^2 > >∴bc2+cg2=bg2>
